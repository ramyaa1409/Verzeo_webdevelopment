About us

The 'tone' of your skin is says the genetically endowed amount of melanin in your epidermis it's called a skintone. If you have good general idea about your surface color it's good for you to buy proper shade of make up products for your skin.But you are struggling to differentiate what type your skintone like fair,mild and dark.

Fair-The lightest range of skin tones.
Mild - You have a tan skin tone with warm golden or olive undertones.
Dark- The deepest range of skin tones.
If you want brief knowledge about your skin tone Just once browse our page upload image or take image then you can easily get your skin tone.
